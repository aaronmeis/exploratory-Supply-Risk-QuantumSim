# Data Sources: Supply-Risk-QuantumSim

## Recommended Dataset
**Kaggle: Supply Chain Disruption and Recovery Dataset**
- Link: [Supply Chain Disruption and Recovery](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shashwatwork/supply-chain-disruption-and-recovery-dataset)
- Description: 100,000 synthetic disruption events across multiple industries and supplier tiers. Includes production impact and recovery timelines.

## Instructions
1. Download the disruption dataset.
2. Model the supplier tiers as a scale-free or random graph.
3. Place data in `data/`.
